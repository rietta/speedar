Net Congestion

Similar to real-life highways, the so called "information superhighway" sometimes has traffic problems.  Depending on the time of day, net congestion can cause serious slowdowns in your internet connection speed.

My Connection Should be Faster!

Speedar can only estimate the speed of your internet connection.  In fact, the results are really the speed between your computer and the Speedar server and not your connection to the internet.  The results can be affected by our connection to the internet, net congestion, and the number of people accessing our server concurrently.

Why is my 56K Modem Only Doing 45k?

No 56K modem ever reaches its full speed potential.  If you are doing anything about 42k, you have about as good a connection as you can get over a phone-line modem.
